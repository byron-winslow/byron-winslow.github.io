I am from a small city in Northern Maine called Presque Isle with a population of about 9,100. Presque Isle is French for "almost Island" and it is also a very French area as it is 15 minutes from the Canadian border.

Presque Isle is surrounded by farms and if you look at a Google Earth image of Maine you can see where it is located because of the discoloration on the map due to a lot of farmlands. Dispite Presque Isle being a farm town it is also the business districk for Aroostook County and a pretty busy city "for Northern Maine standards". Here are some air shots of Presque Isle
